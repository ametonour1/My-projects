Make your employees much happier with our Payroll Cards

Give your employees debit cards under their name and manage them through our unique interface. You can easily manage and track all employee’s expenses. Your pay is automatically loaded onto the card and is available to use right away. It saves time and money and offers employees a better way to get paid (e.g., salary, bonuses, business expenses, etc.) Cash isn’t king when it comes to business spending. Utilizing cash implies either asking staff to pay for expenses themselves – then reimbursing them weeks afterward – or handing out advances. Both are inefficient, that’s why the use of prepaid company cards is a much better solution for your business.

You are a private person and not a business? Great news, We have a perfect solution for you. Manage your account and daily expenses in one place. Instantly access your account balance and use your card abroad anywhere Visa® is accepted. Use contactless payments in-store and withdraw cash from ATMs worldwide. Enjoy the benefits of Apple Pay & Google Pay.
